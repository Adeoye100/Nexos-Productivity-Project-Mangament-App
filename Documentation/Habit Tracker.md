# Habit Tracker

## Overview
Visualize and build streaks for daily, weekly, or custom habits with a GitHub‑style contribution graph.

## Features
- **Year‑long Heatmap** – Each cell represents a day; color intensity reflects completion frequency.
- **Streak Tracking** – Current streak and longest streak displayed for the selected habit or across all habits.
- **Habit Management** – Add habits with name, optional category, frequency (daily/weekly/custom), and color.
- **Edit Past Days** – Toggle edit mode to click past cells and log completion.
- **Today’s Check‑off** – Quick checklist for habits due today.
- **Tooltips** – Hover over a cell to see date and completion details.
- **Vim Navigation** – `j/k` to move between habits, `<Enter>` to select, `<Space>` to toggle today, `/` to add new habit, `Esc` to cancel.
- **Categories & Colors** – Group habits and assign custom colors for visual distinction.
- **Persistence** – All habit data saved in `localStorage`; reset via Settings.
- **Integration** – Completed habits can trigger notifications (see [[Notification Log]]).
