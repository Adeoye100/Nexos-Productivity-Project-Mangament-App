# Security & Privacy

## Overview
Nexos is designed with a "Privacy First" philosophy. Your data belongs to you, and we take significant measures to ensure it stays secure and private within your own environment.

## Key Principles
- **Local-First Data** – Your tasks, habits, and settings are stored in your browser's `localStorage` or a local PouchDB instance.
- **No Backend Collection** – Nexos does not have a centralized database that collects your personal productivity data.
- **Client-Side Encryption** – Sensitive information, such as API tokens, is encrypted on the client side before being persisted.

## Data Encryption
For sensitive integrations (like [[GitHub Integration]]), Nexos uses industry-standard encryption:
- **Algorithm** – AES-256 (Advanced Encryption Standard).
- **Process** – Tokens are encrypted using a browser-unique key before storage.
- **Security** – Even if someone has access to your `localStorage`, your raw API tokens are not exposed as plain text.

## Third-Party Integrations
Nexos connects to external services only when necessary for specific features:
- **GitHub API** – Fetches commit data for the [[GitHub Integration]]. Requests are made directly from your browser to GitHub.
- **Weather API** – Fetches current weather based on your provided location.
- **AI Providers** – If configured, prompts are sent to selected AI models (like Gemini or OpenRouter). Note that your prompts may be processed by these providers according to their privacy policies.

## Data Portability
You have full control over your data:
- **Export/Import** – (Coming soon) Ability to back up and restore your entire dashboard state.
- **Reset** – Clear all local data at any time from the [[Settings Panel]].
